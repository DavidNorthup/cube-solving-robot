# Building the robot.
I created all of these models from scratch using Autodesk Fusion 360 in parametric mode. I printed all of the parts on a Creality CR-10.

## Parts required
* 6 Main Body
* 4 Connector
* 2 Connector Loose
* 6 Cube Turner Tri Fork
* 6 Axel Tri Forks
* 2 Camera Mounts
* 2 Flipped Camera Mounts
